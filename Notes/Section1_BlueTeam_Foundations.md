# Blue Team Fundamentals & SOC Introduction
This section contains my notes and key takeaways from the **TryHackMe SOC Level 1: Blue Team Intro** module.

## 🎓 Key Learnings
* **The SOC Role:** Understanding the hierarchy of L1, L2, and L3 analysts and how they collaborate during an incident.
* **Attack Vectors:** * **Human:** Social engineering, phishing, and the importance of user awareness.
    * **Systems:** Misconfigurations, unpatched software, and lateral movement.
* **Incident Lifecycle:** A day in the life of an analyst (Triage -> Analysis -> Remediation).
